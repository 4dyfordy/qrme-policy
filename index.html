Privacy Policy for QR-Me

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

QR-Me ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the QR-Me mobile application.

2. Data Collection

  • Camera Access: We use your device camera solely to scan QR codes and take photos of your inventory items. Images are stored securely in your private database.
  • AI Processing: We use Artificial Intelligence to assist in tagging and identifying items. We do not use your inventory photos to train third-party AI models.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect a unique device ID to manage your trial period and subscription status.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely via Firebase. We implement industry-standard encryption and do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.
